Biography

Dennis Agyei Asamoah (Hommie Blackuz) is a dynamic Hiplife artist from Ghana,known for blending traditional Ghanaian highlife rhythms with contemporary hip-hop beats. His unique sound captures the vibrant energy of urban life in Accra, making him a favorite among young audiences.Born and raised in kumasi,Hommie began his music career in 2017 with the release of his first single king of the street which earned him the recognition in his vicinity He draws inspiration from the pioneers of Hiplife, such as Reggie Rockstone and Obrafour,while infusing modern Afrobeats elements to stay relevant in the ever-evolving music scene. His lyrics often tackle themes of ambition, love, and the hustle of everyday life,resonating with the struggles and dreams of the youth in Ghana.
